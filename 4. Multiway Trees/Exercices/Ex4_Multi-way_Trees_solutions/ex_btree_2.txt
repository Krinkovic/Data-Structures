Traverse(TREE)

Step 1: IF TREE = NULL
            Return
        [END OF IF]
Step 2: Repeat Steps 3 to 4 while I < TREE->KEY_COUNT
Step 3:     Traverse(TREE->CHILDREN[I])
Step 4:     Write TREE->KEYS[I]
        [END OF LOOP]
Step 5: Traverse(TREE->CHILDREN[TREE->KEY_COUNT])
STEP 6: END
